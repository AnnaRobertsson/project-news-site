Say goodbye to eye strain! We've got expert tips to save your eyes from screen torture!

Eye strain is a common problem that occurs when we spend extended periods staring at screens, such as computers, smartphones, or tablets. Prolonged screen use can lead to symptoms like dry eyes, blurred vision, headaches, and general discomfort. To mitigate these issues, it's essential to follow certain practices to protect our eyes. By implementing these practices, you can minimize eye strain, reduce discomfort, and promote overall eye health despite prolonged screen use. Remember to strike a healthy balance between screen time and other activities to give your eyes ample time to rest and rejuvenate.
